Maintenance ~2900 kcal (BMR 2128 × light activity). Eating 2000 = ~800 deficit. High protein + fiber + water-volume food = kills snacking. Each day = 1 breakfast + 2 mains, rotate freely.

Breakfasts pick one

A · Veg Omelette + Yogurt

~370 kcal · 33g P
  • 3 eggs, spinach + tomato, 1 tsp olive oil (skip butter)
  • 150g Greek yogurt 0–2% + 100g strawberries on side
  • Add a scoop protein in the yogurt → 55g P

B · Yogurt Protein Bowl no cook

~450 kcal · 50g P
  • 250g Greek yogurt 0–2% + 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 banana or 100g berries · optional 30g oats
  • Biggest morning fullness, zero effort

Mains pick two/day

1 · Chicken Thigh + Rice + Big Veg

~800 kcal · 48g P
  • 200g boneless skinless thigh, fresh in pan
  • 60g dry baldo rice (measured — calorie dial)
  • 300g broccoli/carrot/zucchini · 1 tbsp oil total
  • Prepped garlic-yogurt or tomato sauce

2 · Shrimp + Veg Stir-Fry

~680 kcal · 50g P
  • 200g shrimp (4 min, no pre-cook)
  • 400g stir-fry veg — peppers/broccoli/snap peas/onion
  • 1 tbsp oil · prepped soy-garlic-ginger sauce · 60g rice optional
  • Huge volume, leanest protein

3 · Lean Bolognese sauce ahead

~560 kcal · 38g P/portion
  • Batch (5 portions): 500g 5% beef/turkey + 2 onion + 4 carrot + 300g mushroom + 2 cans crushed tomato + garlic/herbs
  • 75g dry pasta per serve
  • Your favorite — fits prep-ahead perfectly

Supplements

Creatine 5g/day, every day, timing irrelevant, no loading. Scale jumps +1–2kg water first 2 weeks — that's not fat, ignore it.

Protein powder to bridge to 180g — breakfast + post-workout.

Tuning

Weigh weekly, morning, post-bathroom. Losing >1kg/wk + feeling drained → add 100g rice or a fruit. Stalled 2–3 weeks → cut a tbsp oil or 20g rice.

Real win: kill pastries, swap butter → measured oil, pile on veg volume. 80% of the result right there.

Built as fuel, not enjoyment — for the next few weeks. You got this. 💪
